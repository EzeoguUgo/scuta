ABOUT US

Smart City Urban Transit App: SCUTA is solving africa's biggest public transport hurdles using our Delux 12 seater Buses, Machine learning that generates Virtual Bus Stations in real time based on your location, Bus Informations/Optimization and Our 2 minutes rule that ensures you never have to walk too far or wait too long to get the most out of everyday. SCUTA is built to provide each user with a fun ride sharing experience on every ride.
